Coloured Squares

A social art project

Coloured Squares is a social art project by Filip Hnízdo. Hi!

It’s very much in early stages (which is mildly worrying as it was started in 2010) but here it is, the first proper public beta release (*deep breath*).

Before I get on to what it actually is (as much as I know), it's worth noting that it would be amazing if you reported any bugs you find on GitHub. There are bound to be many.

It might not even work at all. If so, please get in touch. It can't improve without you.

How should it work?

Coloured Squares is made up of Squarefields, each of which is tied to a user account.

Each squarefield has 256 squares, stacked and ordered together in a grid of 16 by 16 squares.

Each square can be painted or lit in two ways, either with a colour or with an image.

Every change on the site happens as close to real time as the server can handle.

Squarefields can be friended allowing people to collect favourites and make friends. A bit like a social network.

The owner of a squarefield can lock or hide squares from viewing or painting. This can be limited to friends, logged in users, or even just the owner themselves.

What can you do with it?

Anything you want.

Some examples:

  • Paint a picture
  • Display your Instagram photographs
  • Find images of chess pieces and play chess
  • Play whack-a-mole
  • Find images of individual letters and spell out words
  • Make mazes out of locked squares, perhaps with unlocked squares as a prize.
  • Collaborate on something with others.

If you create any of these or anything else amazing, please get in touch so I can point it out.
